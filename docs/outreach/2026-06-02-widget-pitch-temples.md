## Variant — when pitching a US/UK/diaspora temple

Replace "Pranam" with the temple's customary greeting (Namaste, Hari Bol, etc.). Diaspora temples often have a tech-savvy webmaster — emphasise:
- *"Set the timezone to your city — Dallas, London, Sydney — and the panchang reflects sunrise where YOUR congregation is, not Indian sunrise"*
- *"Tithi end-times in your timezone matter for fasting decisions (Ekadashi vrat, Pradosham)"*

## Notes

- **Personalisation is non-negotiable.** "I noticed your site lists upcoming festivals" must be true and specific. Visit each target site for 2 minutes before writing.
- **Don't pitch to temples whose primary mission isn't daily ritual** (e.g., heritage-preservation sites focused on architecture). They won't embed.
- **If they say "yes" but ask for an exception** (no attribution / custom colours not in the picker / etc.) — engage. Each exception costs us a per-deal hour but a meaningful temple embed is worth it.
- **Track the response rate.** If after 15 sends we're <10% reply rate, the subject lines aren't working — A/B different angles.
