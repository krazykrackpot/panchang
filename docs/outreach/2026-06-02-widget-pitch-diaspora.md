## What NOT to say

- Don't compare us to Prokerala / Drik / Shubh by name. Diaspora admins often use those sites — naming them feels combative.
- Don't claim "more accurate than" without proof. Lead with verifiable facts: "Swiss Ephemeris", "9 languages", "any location".
- Don't ask for testimonials or social shares in the first email. Ask only for an embed.
- Don't promise SEO benefits to them — the widget is `noindex`. Their SEO is unaffected (which is actually the point: you're not creating duplicate-content competition).
