# Gemini portrait prompts — Sadhaka Path

Reference for re-generating the 7 level portraits if needed. Paste the shared style key + one subject prompt at a time. Save the output as `public/sadhaka-path/<slug>.png`.

## Shared style key

```
Traditional Indian devotional portrait, aspect ratio 2:3 (tall portrait,
1024×1536 or 832×1248). Single archetypal figure centered, three-quarter
view, dignified posture. Deep midnight navy background (#0a0e27) with
faint cosmic stars and a subtle radial mandala behind the figure. Gold
leaf accents and aura glow in the antique-gold palette (#d4a853 with
highlights #f0d48a and shadows #8a6d2b). Style influences: Pichwai
painting + Pahari miniature + tarot card composition — flat illustrative,
NOT photorealistic. Ornate gold filigree border framing the entire
portrait, with a small empty cartouche at the bottom (the app overlays
the level name there in Devanagari + English, so leave that area clean).
Crisp gold outlines, rich layered detailing on the figure's garments and
ornaments, no text artefacts anywhere in the image. Mood: spiritual,
aspirational, lit from within. Aesthetic continuity with traditional
Hindu deity portraits used in temple art.
```

## Per-level subject prompts

### 1. `shishya.png` — The Student

```
SUBJECT: A young Vedic boy student, clearly TEENAGE (around 14 years old,
beardless, soft features), seated cross-legged on a kusha-grass mat in a
sunlit courtyard under the broad canopy of a peepul tree. He wears
plain undyed white cotton — simple lungi + bare chest with a thin yagnopavita
(sacred thread) over the left shoulder. Hair tied in a small topknot.
Hands resting palms-up on his knees in attentive listening posture
(NOT holding anything — empty handed, that's the point). A small
brass diya at his side glows softly. Dappled morning sunlight through
leaves. Mood: humility, openness, day one of learning.
KEY: Youth + emptiness + outdoor courtyard = beginner. No manuscripts,
no scrolls, no spectacles. He is yet to receive knowledge.
```

### 2. `sadhaka.png` — The Practitioner

```
SUBJECT: A devotee in lotus posture (padmasana) on a low wooden seat
beside a polished brass kalash (water pot) garlanded with marigolds and
mango leaves. A large lit oil lamp (akhand diya) directly in front of
him with a tall steady flame. His eyes are closed in meditation, hands
in dhyana mudra in the lap. Tilak on the forehead. Behind him, a small
yantra etched in gold lines on the wall. The air shimmers faintly with
incense smoke catching gold light.
```

### 3. `jignasu.png` — The Curious Seeker

```
SUBJECT: A young adult seeker (around 30, lean, clean-shaven, intense
eyes), wearing a deep indigo-blue cotton robe with a thin saffron sash.
He kneels on one knee in a DIM CHAMBER — dramatic chiaroscuro, most
of the frame in shadow. He holds a small brass diya HIGH in his right
hand, illuminating a single palm-leaf manuscript he holds in the left
hand at face-level, reading by lamp-light. His face is half-lit, mouth
slightly open as if mid-question. Multiple opened scrolls and palm-leaf
bundles scattered around him in the darkness. Behind him in the dark,
a stylised third-eye yantra etched faintly in gold on the shadowed stone wall.
Mood: discovery, the moment of "but wait — what about…?"
KEY: Indigo robe + chiaroscuro lighting + lamp raised + question on
the face = the seeker who has begun questioning.
```

### 4. `vidvan.png` — The Scholar

```
SUBJECT: A middle-aged scholar (around 45, dark hair and beard with
distinct GREY STREAKS at the temples, wearing brass-rimmed reading
spectacles low on the nose), dressed in deep FOREST-GREEN silk angavastram
draped over the left shoulder with a gold border. He sits at a low
ornately-carved teakwood writing desk, BENT FORWARD over the work,
reed pen in hand, mid-stroke on a palm-leaf folio. The chamber is a
library: tall wooden shelves visible BEHIND him filled with neatly stacked
palm-leaf bundles and bound manuscripts. A bronze inkwell beside the
hand. One large standing lamp throws warm gold light on the desk; the
shelves recede into deeper shadow. Mood: industrious mastery, the
practitioner who has become a scholar.
KEY: Middle-age + spectacles + green robe + library shelves + writing
posture (NOT teaching) = the scholar at work.
```

### 5. `jyotishi.png` — The Astrologer

```
SUBJECT: An astrologer in white silk dhoti and gold-bordered shawl
holding a kundali chart (12-house diamond layout, North Indian style)
in one hand and an astrolabe-like brass instrument in the other. His
gaze is lifted to the sky where the nine planetary glyphs (Sun, Moon,
Mars, Mercury, Jupiter, Venus, Saturn, Rahu, Ketu) hover faintly visible
as small gold orbs in a constellated arc above him. Rudraksha mala
around his neck. A small stack of ephemeris books at his feet. Posture:
mid-calculation, reading the cosmos.
```

### 6. `acharya.png` — The Teacher

```
SUBJECT: An ELDERLY teacher (around 70, fully WHITE long flowing beard
reaching mid-chest, snow-white hair, NO spectacles, gentle creased face),
wearing a pristine ivory-white silk dhoti and angavastram with rich gold
zari borders. He sits perfectly upright (NOT bent over a desk) on a
RAISED ornately-carved wooden dais. His right hand is held HIGH in
jnana mudra (thumb and index touching, other fingers extended), clearly
delivering teaching mid-lecture. Left hand rests on a closed manuscript
in his lap. A bright sunburst halo behind his head. In the FOREGROUND
lower edge of the frame, THREE or FOUR clearly visible students seated
on the floor below the dais — their heads and upper backs facing the
acharya (not us). The hall is bright with multiple oil lamps. Mood:
authoritative warmth, the one who gives knowledge to others.
KEY: Elder + pure white beard + ivory robe + raised teaching pose +
multiple students visible = the teacher whose presence summons disciples.
```

### 7. `rishi.png` — The Sage

```
SUBJECT: A wizened sage with long flowing white hair and beard (jata —
matted dreadlocks coiled atop the head), seated in deep samadhi on a
tiger skin on a high Himalayan peak. Bare-chested with a single saffron
cloth across the waist; rudraksha malas in multiple strands across the
chest. A small kamandalu (water pot) beside him. A serene deer or
peacock companion at the edge of the frame. The sky behind shows the
Milky Way and a single bright north star directly above his crown.
Aura of golden light surrounds his head. The summit — composition
should feel earned, rare, ancient.
```
