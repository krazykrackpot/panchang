## Free AI Video Tools

### Kling AI (recommended for budget)
- **Free tier:** 66 credits/day (~6 minutes of video)
- **Quality:** Very good, handles astronomical/cosmic scenes well
- **URL:** https://klingai.com
- **Best for:** Clips 1, 2, 5, 6

### Pika Labs
- **Free tier:** 3 videos/day, 4s each
- **Quality:** Good for abstract/artistic shots
- **URL:** https://pika.art
- **Best for:** Clips 3, 4

### Luma Dream Machine
- **Free tier:** 5 videos/month
- **Quality:** Excellent for slow cinematic shots
- **URL:** https://lumalabs.ai/dream-machine
- **Best for:** Clip 2 (planets)

### Runway Gen-3 (if budget allows)
- **Free trial:** 125 credits (25 seconds of video)
- **Paid:** $15/mo for 625 credits
- **Quality:** Best overall, but costs money
- **URL:** https://runwayml.com

## Assembly Workflow

```
1. Generate narration → Google Cloud TTS (free)
2. Generate animation scenes → hora-animation.html screen capture (free)
3. Generate B-roll → Kling AI free tier (free)
4. Add background music → YouTube Audio Library (free)
5. Assemble in DaVinci Resolve (free) or CapCut (free)
6. Export 16:9 (YouTube) + 9:16 (Shorts/Reels)
```
