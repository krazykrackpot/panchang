# UX Audit: Newcomer Experience — 15 May 2026

**Trigger:** User feedback from Surya Dwivedi: *"I don't even know what Panchang is. The depth is beyond me. What's the purpose and who is the target?"*

**Analytics context:** 80 visitors, 29 signups (36%), but only 6 generated a kundali (21% of signups). 58% mobile. ChatGPT is #2 referrer (14%). India 58%, international 42%.

**Core paradox:** The CONTENT is excellent — Module 0.1 is genuinely welcoming, the Tippanni readings are personal and accessible, the tools are powerful. But the SURFACE LAYER (homepage, nav, labels, CTAs) is optimised for insiders. The site speaks Sanskrit before it speaks English.

---

## 1. HOMEPAGE

### 1.1 Hero says nothing concrete (CRITICAL)

**What's there:** Sanskrit Gayatri Mantra → "Unveiling the Science of Time" → poetic subtitle → three CTAs.

**Problem:** A newcomer has no idea what this site DOES. "Science of Time" could be anything. The Sanskrit verse at the top signals "this is for insiders" before a single word of explanation.

**Fix:** Replace H1 with benefit-driven copy: *"Free Vedic Astrology — Birth Charts, Daily Forecasts & Auspicious Timing"*. Move Sanskrit to decorative aside. Subtitle should state what users can DO: *"Generate your birth chart, check today's auspicious times, or find the best date for life events — all free, computed for your exact location."*

### 1.2 CTAs use jargon (HIGH)

Three hero buttons: "Today's Panchang" | "Generate Birth Chart" | "Find Shubh Muhurta"

- "Today's Panchang" — newcomer doesn't know what Panchang is
- "Find Shubh Muhurta" — double jargon
- "Generate Birth Chart" — the only one a newcomer understands, but it's the middle button

**Fix:** Make "Generate Birth Chart" the primary CTA (larger, brighter, leftmost). Rename others: "Today's Cosmic Forecast", "Find Auspicious Times".

### 1.3 Panchang widget is the most prominent AND most confusing element (CRITICAL)

The first content section shows 5 cards: TITHI, NAKSHATRA, YOGA, KARANA, VARA — with values like "Trayodashi", "Ashwini", "Ayushman", "Vanija". Zero English explanations visible.

**Fix:** Add one-line English descriptions: "TITHI (Lunar Day)", "NAKSHATRA (Moon's Star)". Show a plain-English summary first: *"Today is neutral for new beginnings. Avoid important work 2:30-4:00 PM. Best window: 9-10:30 AM."* Then show the detailed cards.

### 1.4 No value proposition above the fold (HIGH)

The "What is Panchang?" explainer is the 4th section, well below the fold. The "Science Behind the Sacred" section assumes the reader already cares.

**Fix:** Add a 3-icon benefit bar below the hero:
- **Your Birth Chart** — Personality, career & life patterns from your birth sky
- **Daily Timing** — Best and worst times today for decisions
- **Compatibility** — Traditional 36-point marriage matching

### 1.5 31 unexplained jargon terms on homepage (HIGH)

Full list: Panchang, Kundali, Tithi, Nakshatra, Yoga, Karana, Rahu Kaal, Choghadiya, Muhurat/Muhurta, Graha, Rashi, Jyotish, Dasha, Vimshottari, Shadbala, Ashtakavarga, Tippanni, Varshaphal, Samvatsara, Masa, Paksha, Rahu, Ketu, Dosha, Lagna, KP System, Krishna Paksha, Shubh, Vedic, Nadi, Sudarshana.

**Fix:** Every term needs parenthetical English on first use: "Nakshatra (birth star)", "Tithi (lunar day)", "Dasha (planetary period)".

### 1.6 No trust signals visible above the fold (MEDIUM)

NASA accuracy claim, Meeus algorithms, "free" — all buried in paragraph text below the fold.

**Fix:** Thin trust bar below hero: *"100% Free · NASA-grade accuracy · Used in 40+ countries · No sign-up required"*

### 1.7 No newcomer pathway (HIGH)

No "New to this?" card, no beginner entry point. Module 0.1 is excellent but buried 4+ clicks deep.

**Fix:** "New to Vedic Astrology? Start here →" card in the hero section linking to Module 0.1. Surface a teaser of the opening paragraph.

## 2. KUNDALI PAGE

### 2.1 SEO article wall BEFORE the form (CRITICAL — #1 conversion killer)

A dense article ("What is a Kundali?", house explanations, 12-row HTML table) renders ABOVE the birth form. On mobile, the form is well below the fold.

**Fix:** Move the SEO article BELOW the form or into an expandable "What is a Kundali?" section. The form must be the FIRST thing visible after the heading.

### 2.2 No preview of what you'll get (CRITICAL)

Zero demonstration of what the output looks like. Users are asked to enter personal birth data without seeing the payoff.

**Fix:** Add a "Here's what you'll get" section or static mockup above the form showing: personality profile, career outlook, current period, life domains — with callouts. Single most impactful change for conversion.

### 2.3 No birth time guidance (HIGH)

The time field is required with no helper. Many people don't know their exact birth time — this is the #1 drop-off for kundali tools.

**Fix:** Helper text below the time field: *"Don't know your exact time? Use 06:00 (sunrise) for a general reading — Moon sign and most planets will still be accurate. Only the Ascendant and house positions need precise timing."*

### 2.4 Ayanamsha & Chart Style exposed in form (HIGH)

Ayanamsha dropdown (10 options, pure jargon) and North/South chart toggle are visible before the user has generated anything.

**Fix:** Hide behind "Advanced Options" disclosure. Default Lahiri + North Indian. Show after first generation as a toggle.

### 2.5 Post-generation: technical header first (HIGH)

After generation, the first thing shown is: "Lagna (Ascendant): Sagittarius (260.49°) | Rashi: Aquarius | Nakshatra: Purva Bhadrapada (Pada 1) | Tithi: Chaturdashi (Shukla) | Yoga: Shula" — 6+ jargon terms and 8+ action buttons.

**Fix:** Lead with the interpretive headline: *"Jupiter dominant — this is the defining pattern of your chart."* Push technical details into a collapsible section. Group action buttons into "Share" and "Export" dropdowns.

### 2.6 23 technical tabs visible at once (CRITICAL)

Expert mode shows: Birth Chart, Blueprint, Planet Positions, Dasha Timeline, Ashtakavarga, Varga Analysis, Graha, Yogas, Avasthas, Argala, Sphutas, Shadbala, Bhavabala, Bhava Chalit, Ayanamsha, KP System, Sade Sati, Jaimini, Life Timeline, Remedies, Sudarshana, Nadi Amsha, Patrika.

A newcomer faces complete paralysis. Tab labels use untranslated Sanskrit.

**Fix:** Group into 4 categories: "For You" (Summary, Life Timeline, Remedies) | "Your Planets" (Birth Chart, Positions, Graha) | "Deep Analysis" (Yogas, Shadbala, etc.) | "Advanced" (Jaimini, KP, Nadi). Show only the first category by default. Add English subtitles: "Shadbala (Planet Strength)", "Avasthas (Planet States)".

### 2.7 personalReading failure falls to technical view (CRITICAL)

If the personalReading computation fails, the catch block does `setView('technical')` — dropping a confused newcomer into 23 raw technical tabs with zero guidance.

**Fix:** Fall back to ELI5 (Beginner) panel, not technical view.

### 2.8 "Ask Your Chart" AI hidden at page bottom (HIGH)

The one feature that could rescue confused users — "ask any question in plain English" — is a collapsed `<details>` at the absolute bottom, after thousands of pixels of content.

**Fix:** Floating button or prominent position near the top of Summary view. This is the natural escape hatch for confused newcomers.

### 2.9 Life domains buried below technical content (MEDIUM)

Career, Health, Marriage etc. — the most personally meaningful content — appear after Chart Narrative, Vitality badge, yoga/dosha summaries.

**Fix:** Make life domains the FIRST section after a brief narrative intro.

### 2.10 No "what to do next" takeaway (MEDIUM)

No concluding "Here are your top 3 actions" card. Users finish reading and don't know what to DO.

**Fix:** "Your Top 3 Takeaways" card at the end of SummaryView: most important current insight, one domain needing attention, one practical remedy.

## 3. LEARN SECTION

### 3.1 Module 0.1 is excellent (STRENGTH — preserve this)

"What is Jyotish?" starts from absolute zero. Uses BeginnerNote components, conversational tone, QuickCheck quizzes, concrete analogies. Estimated 10 min, 4 pages. This is the template for all content.

### 3.2 Phase/module titles use unexplained jargon (HIGH)

19 unexplained Sanskrit terms in the first 5 phases: Grahas, Rashis, Ayanamsha, Tithi, Nakshatra, Yoga, Karana, Hora, Muhurta, Lagna, Bhavas, Vargas, Dashas, Vimshottari, Ashta Kuta, Navamsha, Sade Sati, Ashtakavarga, etc.

**Fix:** English parentheticals in LearningPath display: "Grahas (Planets)", "Tithi (Lunar Day)", "Dashas (Planetary Periods)". Add one-line descriptions to PHASE_INFO entries.

### 3.3 Reference Library shown by default — overwhelming (MEDIUM)

3 groups, 100+ link chips visible by default. "260 modules, 17 phases" stat counters are intimidating.

**Fix:** Change `showRefs = false` default. Show only Phase 0-2 by default with "Show all phases" expander.

### 3.4 Standalone learn pages have NO next/prev navigation (HIGH)

Module pages (`/learn/modules/0-1`) have quiz + progress + next button. Standalone pages (`/learn/grahas`, `/learn/rashis`) are dead ends — no next/prev, no progress tracking.

**Fix:** Add footer nav to all standalone pages: "← Previous | Back to Path | Next →". Highest-impact single change for the learn section.

### 3.5 Reference pages jump to data without introduction (MEDIUM)

`/learn/grahas` opens with orbit period tables and combustion degrees. No "What are grahas and why do they matter?" intro. Same for `/learn/rashis`.

**Fix:** Add 2-3 paragraph introduction before data tables: what this concept is, why it matters, how it connects to the birth chart.

### 3.6 Learn-to-tool cross-linking is inconsistent (MEDIUM)

Tool pages link well to learn content (15+ learn links from kundali page). But learn pages don't consistently link back to tools.

**Fix:** Standardised "Try it yourself" CTA at the bottom of every learn page: "See this in action → Generate your birth chart" or "View today's panchang".

## 4. NAVIGATION

### 4.1 Nav items use jargon (HIGH)

Menu: Home, Dashboard, Family, Panchang, Daily Horoscope, Kundali, Rituals, Calendars, Tools, Learn Jyotish

4 of 10 items are unexplained: Panchang, Kundali, Learn Jyotish, Rituals.

**Fix:** Add subtitles or rename: "Kundali (Birth Chart)", "Panchang (Daily Calendar)", "Learn Jyotish (Course)". Or simply: "Birth Chart", "Daily Forecast", "Learn".

### 4.2 Tools page is a wall of jargon cards (MEDIUM)

22 tool cards with titles like Rahu Kaal, Choghadiya, Hora, Dinacharya, Vedic Time, Chandrabalam, Tarabalam — no English descriptions.

**Fix:** One-line description per tool card: "Rahu Kaal — Today's inauspicious period (avoid starting new work)".

## 5. MOBILE CONVERSION FUNNEL (375x812)

### 5.1 Kundali form is 3.2 screens below the fold (CRITICAL — #1 conversion killer)

On mobile, the SEO article consumes ~2000px above the `<KundaliClient>` component. The "Generate Kundali" button is at y=2559 — a user must scroll past 3.2 full screens of educational text to reach the form.

**Fix:** Move SEO article BELOW the client component in `page.tsx`. 5-minute change.

### 5.2 Life domains at screen 7 after generation (CRITICAL)

Post-generation page is 15,024px. Content order: results header (y=1900) → birth chart (y=2833) → Ask Your Chart (y=3488) → key dates (y=4266) → personality (y=4764) → strengths (y=5710) → yogas (y=6144) → **Life Domains (y=7295)**. The personally meaningful content is 6.5 screens down.

**Fix:** Move life domains ABOVE the birth chart diagram and technical details. Or add a sticky section nav bar.

### 5.3 10 action buttons consume 200px of viewport (HIGH)

Post-generation shows 10 buttons (Edit, Save, New, Patrika, Report, Share×2, WhatsApp, Poster, Western vs Vedic) in wrapped rows.

**Fix:** Show only Edit/Save/Share on mobile. Rest behind "More actions" overflow.

### 5.4 No bottom navigation (MEDIUM)

No sticky bottom nav, no floating CTA. After scrolling 15,000px, user must scroll all the way back up to navigate.

**Fix:** Sticky bottom nav: Home, Panchang, Kundali, Calendar, More.

### 5.5 Signup prompt too late (HIGH)

Triggers at 3 page views or 90 seconds. Users who generate a chart (peak engagement) won't have hit 3 page views yet.

**Fix:** Trigger signup immediately after chart generation.

### 5.6 Default location "Mumbai" for non-Indian users (MEDIUM)

Homepage panchang shows "Mumbai, India" when geo headers are missing. Non-Indian users (42% of traffic) see wrong data.

**Fix:** Prompt "Set your location" instead of defaulting.

---

## KEY INSIGHT

The site has a **"last mile" problem**. The computation engine, the yoga detection, the classical accuracy — that's all world-class. But the presentation layer assumes the user already knows Jyotish. Every surface (homepage, nav, forms, results, learn titles) speaks to the expert first and the newcomer never.

**Surya's feedback is the voice of your actual market.** The 23 people who signed up but never generated a kundali probably had the same experience: *"This is impressive but I don't understand what I'm looking at."*

The fix is not dumbing down — it's adding a **translation layer**. Keep all the depth. Just add English labels, plain-language summaries, and a guided path for people starting from zero. The content to support this (Module 0.1, Tippanni readings, life domains) already exists — it just needs to be surfaced earlier and more prominently.
