Design Notes — V2

Full SVG artwork — each archetype gets a unique dramatic illustration. The Sovereign has a crowned lion with golden mane flames. The Transformer has a scorpion with a glowing red stinger. Each card has its own colour palette (gold for fire signs, red for Mars signs, blue for Saturn signs, etc.).

Cosmic Number — 7 · 15 · 10 · 5 displayed big and bold below the archetype name. This is the user's "cosmic coordinates" — Rashi(7) · Nakshatra(15) · Tithi(10) · Dasha Lord(5). Two people with the same cosmic number are born under identical celestial conditions. Numerology nerds will love comparing these.

Data grid — 2×2 grid + full-width transit band at bottom. Frosted glass cells over the artwork. Each cell has a faint number watermark (the numerology number) and colour-coded dimension label.

Per-archetype artwork needed (12 total):

1. Aries/Mesha — The Warrior: armoured warrior with flaming sword

2. Taurus/Vrishabha — The Builder: bull with Earth/mountain motif

3. Gemini/Mithuna — The Communicator: twin figures with flowing script

4. Cancer/Karka — The Nurturer: crescent moon cradling water

5. Leo/Simha — The Sovereign: crowned lion with mane flames ✓

6. Virgo/Kanya — The Analyst: figure with quill and celestial map

7. Libra/Tula — The Harmoniser: balanced scales with cosmic rays

8. Scorpio/Vrishchika — The Transformer: scorpion with glowing stinger ✓

9. Sagittarius/Dhanu — The Seeker: archer aiming at stars

10. Capricorn/Makara — The Architect: makara (sea-goat) with geometric patterns

11. Aquarius/Kumbha — The Visionary: water bearer with light streams

12. Pisces/Meena — The Mystic: two fish in cosmic whirlpool

Comparison mode: Side-by-side cards. Matching data cells get a gold border glow. The cosmic numbers at top make instant comparison possible: "We're both 7-X-X-5 — same rashi, same dasha lord!"

Sharing: These cards are Instagram-story sized (9:16 variant) or square (1:1 variant for WhatsApp). The QR code / watermark drives traffic back to dekhopanchang.com.
